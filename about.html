MORE ROAR! is an entropic synthesiser and degenerative artwork.

The more you play it, the more its sounds degrade, become re-ordered. Every restart, a different rate of degradation. ‘Raw’ sound weathered into noise and silence.

MORE ROAR! is a collaboration between Dirty Electronics and Agnes Cameron with printed circuit board artwork by Tellamont.
